About Me

Life is like riding a bicycle. To keep your balance you must keep moving. -Albert Einstein

I really like what Einstein said, we cannot stop moving forward. Even if you encounter difficulties, keep looking forward. So I chose to come to Algonquin College to study again in the 40th year of my life. I want to learn something about web design,video design. So I choose the program of Interactive Media Design. I think learning this program can enrich my career options in the future.
